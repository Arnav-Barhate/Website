How the service fits together

We usually start with a short conversation, then narrow the work into something manageable. After that, the focus is on the most useful change, not the most complicated one.

1. Review
Look at the current situation, the goal, and the problem area.
2. Focus
Pick the highest-value task and remove extra noise.
3. Deliver
Share clear next steps or a concrete output the team can use right away.
